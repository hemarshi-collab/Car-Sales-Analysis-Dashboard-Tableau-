Project Overview:
This Tableau project analyzes year-to-date car sales performance, pricing trends, and customer preferences across regions, body styles, and brands. 
The dashboard enables stakeholders to understand sales growth and market demand patterns.

Objectives
Track YTD car sales and revenue growth
Analyze pricing trends and YOY performance
Identify best-selling body styles and colors
Compare sales performance by dealer region and company

Key KPIs
YTD Cars Sold
YTD Average Price
YTD Total Sales
YOY Growth Metrics
Sales by Body Style, Color & Transmission
Region & Company-wise Sales Performance

Dashboard Insights
SUVs and Hatchbacks dominate total sales
Pale White and Black are the most popular colors
Consistent weekly growth trend throughout the year
Strong sales concentration in top dealer regions
Certain brands significantly outperform the market average
